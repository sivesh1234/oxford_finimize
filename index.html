We all know that being a student can hit the bank account pretty hard… So why not get paid for something that is minimum effort and doesn’t interrupt whatever you’re getting upto over summer.

What do I have to do?

It’s pretty simple, all you have to do is invite your mates to Finimize by sending them your personal link on all and every platform you wish to. We'll even give you tips on how to do this quickly and effectively.

Your personal link can be copied and sent to anyone and everyone! You can also track your progress and payout using “The Tracker”.

Let's talk numbers

To be paid, you must have a minimum of 10 sign ups

For your first 24 signups you'll get 50p per signup

If you get 25 signups, it will change to £1 per signup (So if you get 25 signups, we’ll pay you £25!... not bad for a few facebook posts!). We’ve had reps do this in under 30 minutes!

The money doesn’t stop there...

If you refer a friend to be a rep who then goes on to get 30+ sign ups, you’ll get a £5 bonus!
